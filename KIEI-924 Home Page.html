Fortune 500 Experience

Having previously worked at Google, I have the understanding of the workings of a large technology company firsthand. I have worked at various roles and in diverse teams ranging from SMB's to Large Customers. I also did a rotation with the People Operations team to better understand how Google hires.

I have also worked at Uber for a brief time prior to Kellogg. I worked in the Trust & Safety team. I co-developed a national program and worked with cross-functional and city leads to launch the same. This experience allowed me to understand how a tap on a mobile phone needs to translate to physical vehicles that need to move around a city to reach customers.

Entrepreneurial Experience

I am certainly an accidental entrepreneur. It all started at the age of 17 when I filed a patent for my invention of a massaging shoe. My next venture was in the apparel space as I bootstraped $50 and converted into six figure revenues over six years organically.

My latest venture is a meta aggregator for taxis and public transportation. I co-founded and led the development of the app and did so in a lean manner. We grew the app to one of top 40 transport apps in India for both Android & iOS.

Education

I pursued my undergraduate degree in finance at the University of Delhi. I graduated amongst the top 5% of the batch and was placed 2nd in the entrance test in the entire country amongst 20,000 aspirants.
