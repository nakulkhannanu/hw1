Tech In Asia

If you live in south Delhi, chances are you think of a car as your own little palace-on-wheels (worth living and dying for), and it really hurts when this comfort is taken away, even for a day (a flat tyre or the odd-even rule, you never know). For spoilt brats like you, I have a suggestion: look into your phone, try the app I am going to tell you about, and give the multiple modes of transport out there a chance. From on-demand cabs and radio taxis to three-wheelers, the metro, and public buses, you will find a wide range of options at your disposal (for a car-lover like you, I’m blanking out walking and cycling info – though there’s that too). Economic Times

Intra-city travel application Instago that helps users compare prices and book the cheapest mode of transport across different categories including on-demand taxis, autos, public transport and self-drive options is raising $500,000 from multiple investors including angel investor Ramit Sethi of Strongbox Ventures. Instago collates information from multiple transport agencies and commuter generated content for real-time information on different modes of transport. The portal also compiles data through API integration with on-demand transport service providers to provide expected time of arrival and other information to the commuters. Read more here:

ET Brand Equity

Wendy's India has partnered with Instago, an upcoming intra-city travel app, to give its customers an opportunity to experience Instago's app and for Instago's customers to experience Wendy's burgers.The two brands Instago and Wendy's have come together in an attempt to reach out to people on the go who are looking out for quality experiences at an affordable price point. Commenting on the new association Arvind Dixit, chief operating officer, Wendy's India said, "We are happy to partner with Instago and make our offering accessible to more customers. We're always keen to engage with potential customers across different platforms and when Instago approached us, we felt the partnership would be a perfect fit." Read more here:
